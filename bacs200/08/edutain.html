TED Talks

Title and Speaker Video and Link Summary
Why I'm done trying to be "man enough"

Justin Baldoni 		"Are you brave enough to be vulnerable? Are you strong enough to be sensitive?"

Justin goes into the harm that toxic masculinity can have on men and on society as a whole and what he's learned throughout his life about himself and what it means to be a man
My daughter, Malala

Ziauddin Yousafzai 		"Why is my daughter so strong? Because I didn't clip her wings."

Ziauddin details his own life along with his daughter Malala's and what lead to her being shot by the Taliban for wanting an education.
Why can't we talk about periods?

Jen Gunter 		"It shouldn't be an act of feminism to know how your body works."

Jen explores the societal tabboos and shame placed on periods and menstruation. She wants to have an open dialogue to reduce the amount of misinformation spread around.
