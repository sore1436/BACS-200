Wealth Gap in America

Video Script/Outline

  1. Wealth inequality in America has grown along with its wealth concentration.
    1. Warren Buffet, Bill Gates, and Jeff Bezos own as much combined as the bottom 50% of Americans.
    2. The Richest American, Jeff Bezos is worth 21 more times than the one in 1982, Daniel Ludwig.
    3. The top 1% owns more than 50% of stock and less than 5% of debt.
    4. Bottom 50% owns only 1% of wealth and 1 in 10 families have a negative net worth.
  2. Racial wealth inequality in the US is also a serious problem.
    1. A typical white family has almost 10 times more wealth than a typical black family
    2. And they also have over 7 times more wealth than a typical Hispanic family.
    3. Black and Hispanic families are twice as likely as white families to have zero or negative net worth.
    4. Homeownership in the US is heavily skewed towards white families.
  3. Gender Wealth Inequality is not decreasing as most people believe.
    1. Women only make up around 30% of the top 10% earners and only 17% of the top 1%.
    2. From 1997 to 2017, the female-to-male earnings ratio has only increased from 74% to 82%.
    3. A gender pay gap still exists in all racial groups and men make more than women in every US industry.
    4. Finally, US Men have about 3 times more retirement savings than women.
  4. Some Proposed Solutions to Americas wealth gap are as follows:
    1. Significantly increase the minimum wage and even guarantee employment.
    2. Raise taxes on the extremely wealthy to decrease the severe concentration of wealth.
    3. Support all forms of housing and also invest in those that are affordable.
    4. Help every day families build rainy-day funds and increase their saved wealth by encouraging early-in-life investments.
